WHY EVERY BUSINESS NEEDS INTERNAL AUDIT?

In your organisation there is devolution of power from top to bottom. This means there are some checks and balances needed for the objective of business to be met. As people join your business they will be having different ambitions. Visionaries will be interested in reaching vision.
