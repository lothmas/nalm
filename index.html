How does NALM help your business to achieve its objective?.

    NALM will be with the business throughout the life cycle of the business, as torch bearer, from cradle to grave will be your advisor and assurer. Fear not NALM assure you success on good corporate governance, risk management and controls. NALM provide board of directors and management with information, appraisals, recommendations and counsel regarding the activities examined and other significant issues.

Why does NALM Internal Audit Solution exist?

    The reason for NALM’s existence is to resolve issues to do with internal audit, making them crystal clear: NALM is an independent, objective assurance and consulting activity designed to add value and improve any business operations. NALM helps businesses to accomplish objectives by bringing a systematic, disciplined approach to evaluate and improve the efficiency and effectiveness of risk management, control and governance processes.

Our Story

Any business should not operate without the knowledge of the risks ahead (future uncertainty events). No matter how small or big your business is you can not afford to operate in the dark about Internal Audit service. So many businesses are falling by the way side because of poor corporate governance, risk management and controls. It is not by mighty nor by power in business that brings success but another very important attribute is attitude towards internal audit. Internal audit can unlock your survival by helping you manage risks.



In the same bush other animals are multiplying but others are going into extinction because of how they manage the hostile or friendly environment. Learn the art to survive in business by properly managing risk. Be adoptive to environment and stay afloat, make use of Internal Audit to navigate to success. How can you prevent risk when you are not aware of it? It is critical that internal audit activity values are understood and accepted throughout the organisation in order to bring a culture of fully utilising it.

Services

WHAT WE DO FOR OUR CLIENTS

  • ◆ Educate our clients about the role of Internal Audit & implementation matrix

  • ◆ We audit effectiveness and efficiency of operations, safety and utilisation of assets, reliability of financial records, compliance to rules and regulations.

  • ◆ We do systems analysis in order to come up with corrupt and fraud proof system,

  • ◆ We come up with Internal Audit Charter for your business giving authority, responsibility and scope to Internal Audit,

  • ◆ We educate clients on how to come up with a conducive environment to address corruption and fraud among other things.

  • ◆ We make recommendations to our clients at the end of audit on how to improve on governance, risk management and control

SOLUTIONS WE PROVIDE

  • ◆ Verify the existence of assets, assess and recommend fully utilisation and proper safeguards for their protection

  • ◆ Evaluate systems and recommend the most profitable system, and check adequacy of the system of internal controls and recommend improvements

  • ◆ Check on compliance with state laws to make your business a solution to community problems.

  • ◆ Assess and advise on good policies, procedures and sound business practices

  • ◆ Assess before, during and after contract benefits and obligation towards business and make appropriate recommendations.

  • ◆ Review operations/programs to ascertain whether results are consistent with established objectives and whether the operations/programs are being carried out as planned

  • ◆ Before any occurrences of an uncertainty like fraud, embezzlement, theft, wasteful among others, Board of Directors and Management should be aware through NALM internal audit, in order to set the correct level of risk appetite depending upon business risk tolerance level.

  • ◆ The issue of risk appetite and tolerance should be understood by Board of Directors and Management so that the risk appetite is tuned to the correct level of risk tolerance that is to a breakeven point.
